About DeepFake Detective

Empowering Truth in the Digital Age

Our Mission

DeepFake Detective is committed to maintaining digital integrity in an age of rapidly evolving AI technology. Our mission is to provide accessible, accurate, and user-friendly tools to detect AI-generated content and help combat the spread of misinformation.

How It Works

Our platform uses state-of-the-art AI algorithms trained on extensive datasets of both genuine and synthetic media. When you upload content, our system analyzes hundreds of features including:

  • Pixel-level inconsistencies
  • Temporal anomalies in videos
  • Metadata analysis
  • Facial biometric verification
  • Audio-visual synchronization checks
